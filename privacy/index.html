Privacy policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Somthingreat helps users follow bodyweight workouts, track progress, and adjust training based on energy, goals, equipment, and recovery needs.

Information we collect

When you use Somthingreat, we may collect:

  • Your email address when you create an account or sign in with Google
  • Your workout goal, equipment preferences, and onboarding answers
  • Your selected energy level and generated workout settings
  • Your workout progress, completed exercises, custom checklists, and activity history
  • Support messages you send through the app

If you sign in with Google, we use Google only to authenticate your account. We do not access your Google Drive, contacts, calendar, or other Google services.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your workout progress
  • Generate workouts adapted to your goals, level, equipment, and recovery settings
  • Improve the app experience
  • Respond to support requests

Where your data is stored

Somthingreat uses Supabase to manage authentication and store app data securely.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to run the app, such as Supabase for authentication and database storage, and Google when you choose to sign in with Google.

Data retention

We keep your account and workout data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and related data at any time.

Your rights

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: somthingreat@kamyameha.com

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
